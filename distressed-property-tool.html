5-step AI-powered research process

1
Seed Data Pull — Export from Cuyahoga Auditor
Download the full parcel roll for all four cities. Filter for: delinquent taxes 2+ years, vacant classification, last transfer 5+ years ago, or absentee ownership (owner address ≠ property address).
Tool: Cuyahoga Auditor → auditor.cuyahogacounty.gov
2
Cross-Reference Code Violations
Take your parcel list and submit FOIA/public records requests to each city's building department for open violation logs. Cross-reference with the NEO CRIS database. Properties on 2+ lists are highest priority.
Tool: NEO CRIS + city FOIA requests
3
AI Enrichment — Score with Claude
Upload your combined CSV to Claude. Score each property by distress indicators (violations + delinquent taxes + vacancy + absentee owner), flag the highest-probability acquisition targets, and draft outreach letters.
Tool: Claude AI → claude.ai
4
Skip Trace & Owner Outreach
For absentee owners and LLC-held properties, run skip traces to find contact info. Use Claude to draft personalized letters addressing the owner's likely pain point — tax burden, code violation costs, estate issues.
Tool: BatchLeads / TLO + Claude AI
5
Drive-By Validation
Confirm boarded or vacant status in person. Photograph and note: boarding type, utility status, lawn condition, mail accumulation, and whether a vacant building placard is posted. Upload photos to Claude for condition assessment.
Tool: DealMachine + field visit + Claude AI
